FAQ

What is StartOps?

StartOps is a community for supply chain and logistics professionals at the top D2C ecommerce companies. Our mission is to be the first place you turn to when you need help solving a problem — for vendor reviews, informative content, and connections with other experts on the network.

Does StartOps cost anything to join?

No. StartOps is free for members. In the future, paid services may be available through the platform.

Who can join StartOps?

StartOps is open to those with logistics and supply chain expertise at top D2C ecommerce startups. If you have questions about eligibility, please email admin@startops.network.

Are vendors allowed on StartOps?

Definitely not at this point. Members may not use StartOps to solicit business, either for themselves or on behalf of others. Members may not be in a sales or BD role at any company that commercially pursues D2C ecommerce companies. Failure to disclose commercial interests may result in the offending member to be banned permanently from the platform.

What is required of members?

StartOps can only succeed if all members are committed to contributing their expertise. For the beginning portion of your membership, you will receive a weekly survey via email. This will help us take a detailed “inventory” of your skills and experiences. We’ve tried to make these surveys quick and painless. From time to time, you may also be asked or required to complete other tasks in order to retain membership. That said — we know you’re incredibly busy, and we don’t take your time for granted. When we do have asks for you, we’ll ensure they are high-value, easy to complete, and don’t take too long.

I want to write for the StartOps blog. How do I do that?

Just drop us a line at admin@startops.network with a pitch for your idea! We’ll make sure your chosen topic is a good fit for the blog. If it is, you can either submit an article yourself, or you can craft an outline and we can have our team write it for you.

Is it possible to get kicked off the platform?

Yes. The easiest way to get kicked off is by being offensive to other members. StartOps maintains a zero tolerance policy towards hateful or profane language or actions on the platform. You can also get removed for violating our policy against soliciting business on the platform. Finally, StartOps reserves the right to remove anyone from the network at any time, without need for cause.
